Intro

Hi! I'm Ryan Mercado, a full-stack web developer located in West Melbourne, Florida. I have experience in both front-end design and back-end architecture, with ample training in the technologies powering web, mobile and desktop applications.

I currently work as a freelance transcriptionist, converting the spoken word to the written word. My work has taught me to rapidly translate thoughts and ideas onto the keyboard, a skill I leverage frequently as I transition into web development.

About Me

My passions have always been intertwined between communication and problem-solving. In my first year of college at the University of Florida, I chose Journalism as my major, hoping to leverage the communication skills I'd cultivated prior to find professional fulfillment. Unfortunately, I found myself starving for the raw task-oriented problem-solving I'd experienced in mathematics and the computer sciences. Now, as I continue to write new web applications and collaborate with my peers, I find my personal passions fulfilled, and I wake up motivated to learn and to create alongside like-minded professionals.

I aim to provide front-end design and implementation services to such a team, as well as back-end API and architecture implementation. I'm experienced in a core set of front-end technologies (HTML/CSS/JS, React, Bootstrap), and I'm continuing to develop my understanding of core back-end technologies (Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL).

When I'm not working, I like to indulge in hobbies that hold room for growth. I'm quite fond of the acoustic guitar, and I try to make the time to write, record, and produce original music. I like to play and read about retro video games, learning about the immense body of technical history involved in their creation. Finally, I'm a sucker for the occasional hike, but my endurance hasn't grown as much as I'd wish.
