Intro

Hi! I'm Ryan Mercado, a full-stack web developer in Tampa, Florida. I have experience in both front-end design and back-end architecture, with ample training in the technologies powering web, mobile and desktop applications.

My professional career began with freelance transcription, where I've worked to take audio and video files from clients and transcribe them to the written word. My work has taught me how to navigate dynamic constraints and guidelines, and how to manage my time to produce high-quality work under tight deadlines.

About Me

I've always held a strong passion for communication. As a freelance transcriptionist, I've learned to translate the idiosyncrasies of spoken language into clear and parsable text. As a web developer, I now strive to communicate the advanced technical concepts of my work as clearly as possible, facilitating a smooth workflow alongside like-minded professionals.

I work to provide front-end design and implementation services, as well as back-end API and database integration. I'm experienced in a core set of the front-end (HTML/CSS/JS, React, Bootstrap, Chakra UI) and back-end (Node.js, Express, Knex, PostgreSQL) technologies used in modern web development, which I've used to build several live applications for a wide variety of target end-users.

When I'm not working, I like to indulge in hobbies that hold room for growth. I'm quite fond of the acoustic guitar, and I try to make the time to write, record, and produce original music. I like to play and read about retro video games, learning about the immense body of technical history involved in their creation. Finally, I'm a sucker for the occasional hike, but my endurance hasn't grown as much as I'd wish.
